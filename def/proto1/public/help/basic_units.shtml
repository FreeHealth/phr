Units

Select or enter the units of measure. Either standard units or a drop-down list of appropriate units will be displayed in the field.

  • If only one unit of measure is possible, the form field will be pre-filled and you will not be able to type within the field.
  • If the field contains a drop-down list of units of measurement, select the units reported in the test results from the list or begin typing the unit abbreviation to see a list of commonly used measurement units.
  • You may also enter your own units of measure in this field.
