Surgery Date

Enter the date the surgery was performed using your preferred date format. Once you type your entry, the PHR will convert the date to the standard format used by the PHR sytem.

If you are not sure about the date, just enter your best guess. If the procedure was recent and you know the month, enter the year and month. The system will accept the following types of dates:

  • the exact date,
  • the year and month, or
  • only the year

For date formatting tips and date entry shortcuts, see how to enter dates in the PHR
