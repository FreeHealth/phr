Colon Cancer Screening

Colon cancer screening is recommended for men and women ages 50 to 75. Screening options include having a colonoscopy every 10 years, flexible sigmoidoscopy every 5 years, or fecal occult blood testing (FOBT) every year.

To read more about colon cancer screening, go to http://www.ahrq.gov/clinic/uspstf/uspscolo.htm.
