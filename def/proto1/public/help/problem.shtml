Medical Condition

Begin by entering the names of all medical conditions. Include all long-term conditions (e.g., diabetes mellitus, congestive heart failure) whether or not they are currently active or being treated (i.e., you should enter leukemia even if it is in remission).

You should also record serious short-term conditions (e.g., a fracture, diabetes during pregnancy), as well as a major symptom if the cause of the symptom is unknown (e.g., fatigue, shortness of breath).

Include as much detail as you prefer, using your judgment to determine what is important to record in your personal health record.
