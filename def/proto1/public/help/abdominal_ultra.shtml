Abdominal Ultrasound

If you have had an abdominal ultrasound to look for an aortic aneurysm, or ballooning of the aorta, please enter the date of that study. The U.S. Preventive Services Task Force recommends this study be done once for men between the ages of 65 and 75 who have ever smoked because they are at higher risk for aneurysms. See http://www.ahrq.gov/clinic/uspstf/uspsaneu.htm.
