Pap Test

If you are a woman and have had a cervical Pap smear, enter the date of that last study here.

You can also record information about the result. If your doctor told you to come back for another study at a specific time or earlier than usually required, you can also record that date here.

You can read more about cervical Pap testing at http://www.ahrq.gov/clinic/uspstf/uspscerv.htm .
