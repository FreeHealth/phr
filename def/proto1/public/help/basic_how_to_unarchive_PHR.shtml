Unarchive a PHR

To unarchive a PHR

  1. Click the View Archived PHR Records link at the bottom of the PHR Management page. The system will display the Archived PHRs form.
  2. Click the Restore button next to the archived PHR you wish to unarchive.
  3. The system will display a message confirming that the unarchived PHR has been restored to the list of active PHRs.
  4. Click the Return to the list of active PHRs link to view the PHR in the list of saved PHR records.
