What kind of information should I enter?

All fields highlighted with are required fields.

Date EnteredEnter the date the question was entered into the PHR.
CategoryChoose the category of the question to ask or enter your own.
StatusIndicate Not Asked for questions that still need to be answered and Asked for questions which are no longer relevant or have been answered.
QuestionEnter any information needed to remind yourself of the question you need to ask.
AnswerEnter the answer given to your question.

Why is this important?

Keeping a list of questions to ask your doctors will help them properly assess your condition and ensure that you receive the most effective treatment. It is important to discuss all of the questions you have about your symptoms, tests, treatment alternatives, medications, risks, and outcomes with your physician as part of your overall health care plan.
