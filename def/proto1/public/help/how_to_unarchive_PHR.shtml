Unarchive a PHR

To unarchive a PHR

  1. Click the Archived PHRs link at the bottom of the PHR Management page. The system will display the Archived PHRs form.
  2. Select Unarchive PHR Record from the Archived PHR Task drop-down list.
  3. Select the PHR you wish to unarchive from the Archived PHR Record drop-down list.
  4. Click the Go button.
  5. The system will unarchive the PHR and place it back in the PHR Record Name drop-down list of existing PHRs on the PHR Management page.
