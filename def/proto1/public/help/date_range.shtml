Date Range

By default, all dates for previously entered test results and health trackers are displayed; however, you can limit the range of dates you wish to display. For example, if you only want to see information from the past year, choose the Last 1 Year option from the drop-down list of date ranges.

If you want to show the information from a specific time period, choose Customize from the drop-down list and enter the beginning and end dates for the information you want to see.
