Add Test Results and Trackers

Add new medical tests or health trackers to your PHR by browsing or searching for them. The PHR system will generate a customized form for nearly all medical tests and common health trackers.

What do I need to know about adding test results and health trackers?

  • To browse for new tests for trackers
    • Click the Browse Panels/Tests radio button and choose a category from the Category drop-down list.
    • View all of the available tests in that category by clicking on the Panel/Test drop-down list.
  • To search for new tests and trackers
    • Click the Search Panels/Tests radio button and begin typing in the space provided.
  • Once you locate the test or tracker you wish to add, click the Add button.
  • To view the test and all previous tests, select the View & Edit Results Timeline link on the main PHR page. You must add at least one test measurement for the test or tracker to appear as an entry on the View & Edit Results Timeline page.
  • In some cases, not all fields within a test panel are displayed by default. If more fields are available, a Show more arrow will appear below the data entry line in the form. Click the Show more arrow to show additional fields or the Show less arrow to hide additional fields.
