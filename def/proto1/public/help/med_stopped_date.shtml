Stopped Date

Unless you are still taking the medication, enter the date you stopped taking the medication using your preferred date format. Once you type your entry, the PHR will convert the date to the standard format used by the PHR sytem.

Enter the exact date if known; however, the system will accept the following types of dates:

  • the exact date, or
  • the year and month

For date formatting tips and date formatting shortcuts, see how to enter dates in the PHR.
