Delete a PHR

To permanently delete a PHR

  1. First, make the PHR inactive by navigating to the PHR Home page and clicking the Make Inactive button beneath the name of the PHR you wish to delete.
  2. Once the PHR has been made inactive, it will be moved to the Inactive PHRs I Manage section of the PHR Home page.
  3. Click the Inactive PHRs link at the bottom of the PHR Home page to see a list of all inactive PHRs.
  4. Click the Delete button next to the name of the PHR you wish to delete.
  5. The system will prompt you to confirm deletion of the selected PHR.
