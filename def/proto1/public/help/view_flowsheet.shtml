View Test Results and Health Trackers

The View & Edit Results Timeline page allows you to view a summary of your test results and health trackers as either a timeline chart or a graph. The timeline and graph views allow you and your health care provider to quickly and easily track test results such as blood pressure, cholesterol, weight, and others over time.

To view the View & Edit Results Timeline page from the PHR Home page:

  1. Click the button under the name of the PR you wish to view
  2. Next, click the Show Results Timeline button on the View & Edit Results Timeline page.

To view the View & Edit Results Timeline page from the PHR Record Details page:

  1. Select View & Edit Results Timeline link at the top of the PHR Record Details page.
