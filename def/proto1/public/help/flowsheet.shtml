View & Edit Results Timeline

The View & Edit Results Timeline page allows you and your health care provider to quickly and easily track test results such as blood pressure, cholesterol, weight, and others over time. Use this page to:

  • Display a summary of your test results and trackers as either a timeline chart or a graph.
  • Add new test or tracker results to your saved tests or health trackers
  • Edit test or tracker results you have previously entered
  • Delete test or tracker results you have previously entered
  • Print all of your test results

What do I need to know about viewing my test results and health trackers?

  • To view test results from all of your saved tests or health trackers, click the Select All checkbox or select one or more tests or health trackers from the list.
  • Click the Show Results Timeline button to display the selected results.
  • Click on the graph icons next to individual measurement results to view them as a graph.
    Learn more about viewing test results as graphs.
  • The Show Panel Info button allows you to view or hide the Comments, Where Done, and Due Date fields for tests and trackers.
  • The Show Empty Rows button allows you to view or hide all empty test value rows within the test or tracker.
  • The Add Test Results & Trackers button takes you to the Add Test Results & Trackers page to search or browse for new tests or trackers.
