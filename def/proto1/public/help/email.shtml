Email Address

We need an email address to help you recover your PHR account ID and/or password in the event you forget your account information and cannot remember the answers to your security questions.

If you wish to remain completely anonymous, you should create an anonymous email address from a web-based email service provider (such as Google, MSN Hotmail, or Yahoo) to use only for your PHR account. In order to remain anonymous, the e-mail address should not contain any identifying information (such as your name or birth date), and you should not use it to send or receive either personal or other e-mail messages outside the PHR.
