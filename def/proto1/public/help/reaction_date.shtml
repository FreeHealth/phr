Started

Enter the date when you first experienced symptoms of an allergic or dangerous reaction using your preferred date format. Once you type your entry, the PHR will convert the date to the standard format used by the PHR sytem.

The system will accept the following types of dates:

  • the exact date,
  • the year and month, or
  • only the year

For date formatting tips and date entry shortcuts, see how to enter dates in the PHR
