I Can't Remember My PHR Account ID

If you have forgotten your PHR Account ID, we can send it to the email address you supplied when you created your PHR Account.

To have your PHR Account ID sent to your email address

  1. Click the Forgot your account ID? link in the middle of the PHR login page and follow the steps to recover your Account ID.
  2. Supply the answers to the security questions you created answered when you established your account.
  3. If you do not have access to the email address associated with the account, send an email to us using the PHR Support link at the bottom of the PHR page.
