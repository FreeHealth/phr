User Functions and Shortcut Keys

The following table of user functions and shortcut keys provides alternative methods of navigation and data input that will allow you to minimize typing when creating or updating PHRs.

Function Click on(icon) Key short cut Comment
Information links
Access field help Help None A Help link appears adjacent to every field name. The system will display a pop-up help text window when you click this icon.
Dismissing pop-ups; including field help, information buttons, and reminders Close button
 None  
Obtaining clinical information specific to a problem, drug, allergy (and most other items chosen from a menu) Info Page link None Click the link under the Info Page column adjacent to the name of a medical condition, drug, test, allergen, or other item selected from a menu and the system will link to a page of information about the item.
Navigation
Move to next (R to L) open field in form Click on the field of interest with the mouse. Tab Form navigation is from left to right and then down (as on a typewriter)
When you get to the last viewable field on a given screen, the focus of the navigation returns to the top field of the form.
Return (back up) to the previous field (L to R) Click on the field of interest with the mouse. Shift+Tab  
Miscellaneous
Storing the information in a form Press the Save button at the bottom of the form Ctrl+Return  
Editing
Cut and paste   Highlight with shift keys, Ctrl+X to remove, Ctrl+C to copy, Ctrl+V to paste somewhere else in the current form or another program.  
Undo Highlight the text with your mouse and press the Backspace key. Ctrl+Z Changes the contents of the field to the previous value.
