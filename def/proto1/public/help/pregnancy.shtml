Pregnancy

Women of child-bearing age are asked about pregnancy because it is important to minimize certain treatments, medications, and tests (like x-rays) during pregnancy.

What do I need to know about the Pregnancy section of the PHR?

Informing your health care providers of your pregnancy status is an essential step in helping them determine the appropriate medical screenings and treatment of your pregnancy and medical conditions.

What kind of information should I enter?

PregnantSelect Yes or No from the drop-down list.
Due DateIf known, enter the date the baby is due.

Why is this important?

If you are pregnant, think you may be pregnant, or planning to get pregnant, talk to your health care provider about steps you can take for a healthy pregnancy and a healthy baby. See NLM's MedlinePlus Web page for trusted information on Pregnancy.
