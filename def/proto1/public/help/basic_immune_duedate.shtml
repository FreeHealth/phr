Next Due

Enter the due date for the next vaccination in this series if relevant. Leave this field blank if there are no more doses of this vaccine to be given.

The date must be entered in the following format: YYYY/mm/dd. The system will accept the following types of dates:

  • the exact date,
  • the year and month, or
  • only the year

Some vaccines require a series of two or more doses to provide full immunity from disease. Other vaccines, such as influenza, are given at regular intervals (e.g., yearly). Use this field to record the specific due date, or the month and year, for the next dose.

To view a complete list of your drug refill dates, vaccine and appointment reminders, and test due dates, click the Date Reminders link at the top of the PHR page.
