Make a PHR Inactive or Prepare It for Deletion

PHRs that you are no longer using can be made inactive to keep them permanently or to prepare them for deletion. Inactive PHRs will be moved to the Inactive PHRs list at the bottom of the PHR Home page and will no longer appear in the list of PHRs in the My Personal Health Records section. Inactive PHRs can also be made active again.

To move a PHR to the Inactive PHRs section of the PHR Home page

  1. Click the Make Inactive button below the PHR name on the PHR Home page.
  2. Select the PHR you wish to make inactive from the list in the PHR Record Name field.
  3. Click the Go button.
  4. The PHR will appear in the list of inactive PHRs in the Inactive PHRs section at the bottom of the PHR Home page.
