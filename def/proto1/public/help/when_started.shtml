When Started

Enter the date when the condition began using your preferred date format. Once you type your entry, the PHR will convert the date to the standard format used by the PHR sytem.

Enter the exact date if known; however; the system will accept any of the following date elements:

  • the exact date,
  • the year and month, or
  • only the year

For date formatting tips and date entry shortcuts, see how to enter dates in the PHR.
