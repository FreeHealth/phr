How Do I Change My Password?

To change your password

  • Log in to the PHR with your current password.
  • Click the Account Settings link at the top of the right side of the PHR Home page.
  • Follow the instructions for changing your password. You will be prompted for your current password prior to entering a new password. Your password must be at least 8 characters long and include at least 3 of the following: capital letters, lower case letters, numeric characters, or special characters. Your password is case-sensitive.
