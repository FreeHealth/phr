View or Edit an Existing PHR

To view or edit an existing PHR

  1. On the PHR Management page, click the View/Edit link for the PHR you with to edit. The PHR system will then display the PHR Record page.
  2. To add new information to a section of the PHR, click any link except for the Health Reminders and Date Reminders links. (These sections of the PHR are used by the system to display health reminders based on your age, gender, and other risk factors and date reminders for upcoming medical appointments and prescription refills.)
  3. To save your changes, click the Save button at the bottom of the input form.
