Why Stopped

Enter the reason the medication was stopped.

If you have stopped taking the medication, the medication should be marked with the Stopped status. The system will provide you with suggested reasons, such as Allergy, Could not tolerate side effects, or Problem gone; however, you may type your own explanation.

Note that if you enter your own explanation, the background will change color and text will become italicized, signaling that the text you have entered was not found in the list.
